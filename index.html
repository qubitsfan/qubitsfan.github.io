夫夷以近，则游者众；险以远，则至者少。
Flat and close places, more people come to visit.
Dangerous and far places, fewer people come to visit.
而世之奇伟、瑰怪，非常之观，常在于险远，而人之所罕至焉，故非有志者不能至也。
But the wonderful, strange and extraordinary landscapes of the world are often found in places that are remote and seldom visited.
So people without motivations can not see them.
